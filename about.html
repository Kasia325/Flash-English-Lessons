How do I teach?

  • I use screen-sharing to show you interactive and discussion-stimulating slides.
  • We have a conversation based on these slides. I make notes whilst listening to you and we feedback on good language and mistakes after each slide.
  • I pay attention to fluency and coherence, grammar and pronunciation.
  • We're also going to learn and revise any new vocabulary related to the topic of our lesson.
  • We wrap up each lesson with a little summary and revision to make sure that the new material stays in your head!
